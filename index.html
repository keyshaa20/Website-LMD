Study Abroad Opportunity
at UIN Sunan Gunung Djati Bandung

UIN Bandung offers opportunities for students to study abroad. This program is designed to improve the quality of education and open international horizons. UIN Bandung has collaborations with various universities abroad. Through the student exchange program, students can study at partner universities, while remaining registered at UIN Bandung.

Student Mobility program UIN Sunan Gunung Djati

The student mobility program is a program that allows students to study and experience at other universities abroad. This program is usually carried out with the home campus which already has cooperation.

REQUIREMENTS

  • Undergraduate students in semester 4 (S1)
  • Recomendation from Dean
  • Motivation Letter (in English) min 300 words
  • Transcript min 3.25 GPA
  • Score minTOEFL ITP: 450/PTE akademik 36/ielts 5.0/iBT 45/TOEFA UIN 500/ duolingo60
  • Have never received funding support for international program from UIN Bandung / Kementerian Agama
  • Willing to be a volunteer for the International Office

    • WHAT WILL BE COVERED?

  • Return airfare
  • Program
  • Accommodation and meals during the program
